Burst Cherry Tomato Pasta

Description

A simple sauce of juicy, bursty cherry tomatoes that comes together in around 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 6 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 2 lbs Cherry Tomatoes
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium low with a coat of olive oil on the bottom.
  2. When oil is warm add garlic and cook until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, red pepper flakes, basil, and salt and cook until tomatoes begin to burst. Approx. 5 minutes
  4. Lower heat and continue to cook for 10-15 minutes or until tomatoes are broken down to your desired amount. Stirring occasionally.
  5. While tomatoes are cooking down cook pasta according to its directions
  6. Drain pasta and add to tomato mixture.
  7. Top generously with parmesan and serve
