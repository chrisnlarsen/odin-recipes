Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is an Italian antipasto consisting of grilled bread often topped with olive oil and salt. Most commonly it is served with toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, and/or cheese.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatos, Diced
  • Basil
  • Balsamic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut baguette into 1/4 slices and brush with olive oil
  2. Grill or toast the baguette
  3. Mix together tomatoes, basil, Balsamic, olive oil, salt, and pepper
  4. Top toasted baguette with tomato mixture
